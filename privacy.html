Overview

WeatherWatch is a private automation tool used to publish approved weather advisories and related public information content to authorized Facebook Pages.

Information We Access

WeatherWatch may access basic Page information, Page identifiers, Page access tokens, and publishing permissions granted by the authorized Page administrator.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to identify authorized Pages, publish approved posts, and verify that posts were successfully created.

Information Sharing

WeatherWatch does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Data Retention

Access tokens and configuration data may be stored only as needed to operate the service. Access may be removed or revoked at any time by the Page administrator through Facebook settings.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact the WeatherWatch administrator through the contact page.
